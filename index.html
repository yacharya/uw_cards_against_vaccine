Welcome to the Cards Against Vaccine Game!

Can you become the Master Manipulator?

Instructions:

  • The Master Manipulator will click on a Black card with an incomplete headline to select it for the round
  • The rest of the players must decide which of the White cards allocated to them best fill in the headline
  • It’s then up to the Master Manipulator to choose who they think has created the most misleading headline!
  • Players can make their case—remember to leverage the common misinformation tactics in your headlines and explain to the Master Manipulator why people would fall for your persuasive tricks!
  • The Master Manipulator awards points by clicking on the White card(s) they deem to be the most manipulative

    To begin, type the number of players into the box below and click “Start Game”

Round Two

For Round Two, you’re working as a team! Instead of being a “Master Manipulator”, you’ll work together to identify the tactics used by the headline and pick an appropriate “prebunk” to engage in productive conversation.

Instructions:

  • Read through all the cards to get a sense of what cards might go together!
  • The team will pick a black headline they want to debunk
  • Pick the white tactic card you think the headline best utilizes
  • Finally, decide on a grey “grounding truth”
  • Discuss all the three together - do they provide a compelling, non-antagonistic route to engaging with misleading or non-factual information?
  • Once you’ve decided, click “Submit” to see if you’ve successfully debunked the headline!
